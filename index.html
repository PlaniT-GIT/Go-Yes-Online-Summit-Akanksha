About Us

Akanksha Charitable Trust (R.), Puttur [Team ACT]

Nelson Mandela once said, "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world". Accordingly, providing quality education for all stands very crucial in every nation of the world.A greater number of students in India are deprived of proper education because of different circumstances. Holding onto these thoughts adventure of Akanksha has embarked with a group of friends aiming to create a platform for passionate young minds to explore their inner self, discover hidden talents and enrich their journey towards a glorious future. 

Today the team has more than 130 youths working towards the same cause reaching 1700+ youths across the globe. 

Even though there has been lots of advancements in the field of education from traditional classroom learning to present online education, there are numerous hurdles in providing quality education across the world. The complications in furnishing skills, morals, values along with academic knowledge is changing as there are huge technological developments. Accessibility and feasibility of online education is also the important topic to think over. Difficulties in achieving the growth of education field all over the world need to to be discussed and hence Akanksha Charitable Trust Puttur has come up with Global Online Youth Education Summit 2021. The aim being, bringing together young enthusiasts from different backgrounds to analyze the various aspects of present education system. Participants will be given chance to interact with experienced educationalits and hence can find new ideas to implement. Here is the great opportunity to the youth to engage yourself in the discussions of finding solutions to the various issues concerning SDG 4 I.e, "Quality Education".

Highlights of GOYES 2020

  • 01 Delegates from 74 different countries
    Delegates from various parts of the world joined together online for a common cause. Ideas and thoughts from 74 different countries illuminated the minds to ponder over different issues and complications in the education system all over the world. Enthusiastic representatives of respective countries involved themselves very actively in finding the innovative solutions to certain obstacles in the field of Education.
  • 02 Unconference
    Unique set of skills possessed by an individual can be unleashed if the right platform is provided. In an unconference, participants can learn and share their knowledge with each other. All the delegates from different backgrounds and experiences got an opportunity to discuss their ideas in a team and present it to everyone.
  • 03 Eminent Educationalists

    Vocalization of different educational scenarios was of prime importance for GOYES 2020. Renowned speakers with a bundle of experiences and knowledge over the subject “Quality Education for all” spoke their hearts out with real life instances. Thought provoking conceptualization and interactive sessions were the major attraction.

  • 04 Delegate Speakers

    Vocalization of different educational scenarios was of prime importance for GOYES 2020. Renowned speakers with a bundle of experiences and knowledge over the subject “Quality Education for all” spoke their hearts out with real life instances. Thought provoking conceptualization and interactive sessions were the major attraction.

  • 05 Hackathon

    Hackathon on Online platform gave a unique outlook to the conference. Participants were divided into 20 teams and they came up with remarkable solutions for the flaws in the education system. Mentors were allotted to the teams to guide them in the right direction. Presentations were significant and apt to the current situations.

  • 06 Fun Activities

    Engaging in amusing activities helps in boosting up energy. To make the conference enjoyable and memorable, fun activities were introduced in between.

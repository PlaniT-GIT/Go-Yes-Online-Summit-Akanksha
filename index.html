About Us

Akanksha Charitable Trust (R.), Puttur [Team ACT]

Provides equal opportunities for Children who are disadvantaged

In India, majority of rural school children are deprived of quality education due to various factors. Keeping those challenges in mind, the journey of Akanksha embarked with a group of friends who were motivated by the famous quote- "Knowledge never decreases by being shared " and therefore decided to share the knowledge amongst the needy students. Through Interactive Science sessions, counseling, and motivational talks by eminent personalities to the Rural Government school students, impACT-30 leadership tasks in 9 districts of Karnataka, various skill enhancement programs for youth, connecting 17,000+ youth/ students, opening up a Covid Help Centre during the pandemic, Akanksha is standing out of the crowd by finding true happiness coming from the effort of making others happy.

    OUR VISION

  • To enlighten the rural government school children with the advanced scientific knowledge and social awareness; enhance the life skills and leadership in the youth and thereby leading them into mainstream to enjoy the bliss of competitive world.

    • OUR MISSION

  • To inspire and involve youth in service activities along with their self- development, with the aim of disseminating knowledge, encouraging scientific temperament in rural school children, enhancing the youth skills and potentials, and creating hopes for better life.

About this Event

Education, an essential element for the overall development of a human being, has a great potential to change the course of society. But today’s education lacks quality, which makes it less effective. In this regard, this summit will gather people around the globe in a common platform through live sessions and interactions with eminent educators to discuss the various aspects of quality education.

To all the youth out there, if you think you could be the change maker, then here is a chance!
